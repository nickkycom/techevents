In Decline: Global Technology Events Move Online

As technologies develop and broadband connections accelerate, "offline" technology fairs tend to lose their exhibitors and live attendees. At least, they are perceived to do so. But is it fair assessment? Let's explore the facts.

Mobile World Congress

Mobile World Congress (MWC) is the biggest event in the world for all things mobile. It started in 2008, but in fact existed from 2001, when the first 3GSM Congress took place. Companies from all accross the globe present their new products and exhibit technology advancements there.

 

Since 2006 MWC takes place in Barcelona, a picturesque city on the Mediterranean shore. Attendance: 72534 - 2013, 67000 - 2012. Area: 94,000 m2 - 2013), 70500 - 2012. Exhibitors: 1700 - 2013, 1500 - 2012

 

Participants: Samsung, Sony, Ericsson, Huawei, Nokia, AT&T, Vodafone
