<IMG CLASS="descimg" ALT="logo CEBIT" SRC="img/cebitlogo.png"><STRONG class="bigger"><a target=_new href=http://www.cebit.de/home>CEBIT</a></STRONG> is one the largest  technology trade fairs. It's became an independent show in 1986, after being a part of Hannover Fair since 1970. 
The event is held each spring in Hannover, Germany. In translation from German, CEBIT stands for the Center for Office Automation, Information Technology and Telecomunication.
<BR>&nbsp;<BR>
In case you won't be able to attend the main event, there's a bunch of regional CEBIT's throughout the year and around the globe: in Asia (Shanghai), Australia (Sydney) and Eurasia (Istanbul).
